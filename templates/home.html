How the Network Connects Us

Each role in our network performs a crucial part of the food waste rescue ecosystem.

1. Donors

Restaurants, supermarkets, and catering companies easily upload edible food surplus details, expiry times, and pickup locations.

2. NGOs

Shelters, soup kitchens, and non-profits browse available donations in real time and request pickups to feed those in need.

3. Volunteers

Deliver heroes claim delivery tasks, coordinate directly with donors and NGOs, and transport meals to their final destinations.

4. Admins

Verify users, monitor activities, track performance charts, and ensure seamless delivery coordinates across the system.

Global Focus

Our Mission: UN SDG Goal 2

UN SDG Goal 2 focuses on ending hunger, achieving food security, improving nutrition, and promoting sustainable agriculture.

One-third of all food produced globally goes to waste, while millions of people go hungry. By connecting food surplus straight to local shelters, we reduce landfill waste, carbon emissions, and hunger simultaneously.
