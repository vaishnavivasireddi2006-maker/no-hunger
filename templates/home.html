How It Works

Four Roles. One Mission.

Every person in our network plays a vital role in getting food to those who need it most.

Step 01
Donors

Restaurants, supermarkets & households upload surplus food with expiry times and pickup locations.

Step 02
NGOs

Shelters and non-profits browse available donations in real time and submit pickup requests.

Step 03
Volunteers

Community volunteers claim delivery tasks and transport food from donors to NGOs.

Step 04
Admins

Verify users, monitor all activity, track analytics, and keep the network running smoothly.

Global Focus

Aligned With UN SDG Goal 2

SDG Goal 2 calls for an end to hunger, food insecurity, and all forms of malnutrition by 2030. One-third of all food produced globally is wasted — while 700 million people go to bed hungry every night.

By connecting surplus food directly to local NGOs, we reduce landfill emissions, lower carbon footprints, and put nutritious meals on the tables of people who need them.
